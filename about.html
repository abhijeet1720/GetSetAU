About GetSetAU

GetSetAU was created with the mission to make the transition to life in Australia easier and more enjoyable for international students.

We provide practical, trustworthy, and up-to-date information about settling, studying, working, and thriving in Melbourne and beyond.

Whether you are preparing for your journey, looking for a job, or exploring the city, GetSetAU is here to guide you every step of the way.
