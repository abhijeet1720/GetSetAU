Banking & SIM Cards

Opening a Bank Account

Having an Australian bank account is essential for managing your money, receiving payments, and paying bills.

  • Popular banks: Commonwealth Bank, ANZ, Westpac, NAB.
  • You can open an account online before arriving or visit a branch after you arrive.
  • Documents needed: Passport, visa, and proof of address (some banks accept your university enrollment letter).
  • Ask about student accounts with no monthly fees and free international transfers.

Choosing a SIM Card

Staying connected is important. Choose a SIM card that fits your data needs and budget.

  • Popular providers: Telstra, Optus, Vodafone.
  • You can buy SIM cards at airports, convenience stores, or online.
  • Prepaid SIMs are flexible with no contract commitments.
  • Check for student discounts or plans tailored for international students.

Tips for Banking & SIM Cards

  • Set up internet and mobile banking for easy access.
  • Keep your bank cards safe and notify your bank if lost.
  • Compare SIM plans carefully before buying — check data limits and coverage.
  • Some providers offer apps to monitor usage and top-up easily.
