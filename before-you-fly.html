Before You Fly

Important Documents

  • Valid Passport and Australian student visa
  • Offer letter and enrollment confirmation from your university
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) details
  • Accommodation details or temporary address
  • Emergency contact information

Packing Tips

  • Pack weather-appropriate clothing (Melbourne’s weather can be unpredictable!)
  • Bring necessary electronics and adapters
  • Carry any important medications with prescriptions
  • Keep essential documents handy in carry-on luggage
  • Don’t forget chargers, toiletries, and some snacks

Other Tips

  • Inform your bank about your travel dates
  • Arrange airport pickup if possible
  • Research local laws and customs
  • Download useful apps for navigation, communication, and transport
