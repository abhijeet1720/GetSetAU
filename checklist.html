First 30 Days Checklist

Your essential checklist to help you settle smoothly in Australia within the first month.

  1. Arrive & Settle In: Find accommodation and get familiar with your neighbourhood.
  2. Get Your Myki Card: Purchase and top up your transport card for Melbourne public transport.
  3. Open a Bank Account: Set up your Australian bank account for salary and expenses.
  4. Buy a SIM Card: Choose a mobile plan that suits your needs.
  5. Enroll & Attend Orientation: Register at your university and attend orientation sessions.
  6. Apply for Medicare (if eligible): Register for healthcare services if your visa allows.
  7. Find Part-Time Work: Start searching and applying for jobs suitable for students.
  8. Get a Tax File Number (TFN): Apply online to legally work and pay tax in Australia.
  9. Set Up Essentials: Arrange utilities, internet, and familiarize yourself with local shops.
  10. Stay Safe & Connected: Learn emergency numbers, connect with student groups and support services.
