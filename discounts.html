Student Discounts & Budgeting

Maximizing Student Discounts

Many stores, services, and entertainment venues offer discounts for students. Always carry your student ID and ask if discounts are available.

  • Discount Cards: Consider getting a Student VIP card or your university’s discount programs.
  • Transport Discounts: Use student concession fares on public transport with your Myki concession card.
  • Shopping & Food: Some cafes and shops offer “student specials” or discounted prices during certain hours.
  • Entertainment: Movie theaters, museums, and events often have student rates.

Budgeting Tips

  • Track your expenses using apps like Mint, Pocketbook, or even a simple spreadsheet.
  • Set a weekly or monthly budget and stick to it.
  • Cook at home instead of eating out to save money.
  • Look out for free or low-cost events around Melbourne.
  • Buy second-hand textbooks and goods where possible.
