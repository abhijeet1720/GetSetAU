First Week in Australia

Get everything sorted in your first week: banking, SIM card, Myki card, and more!

Checklist for the First Week

🏦 Open a Bank Account

Visit a nearby bank branch (Commonwealth, ANZ, NAB, or Westpac) with your passport and student ID. Ask for a student account with no monthly fees.

📱 Get a SIM Card

Buy a prepaid SIM from providers like Optus, Telstra, Vodafone, or Amaysim. Compare plans and activate with your passport details.

🚇 Get a Myki Card

Purchase a Myki card from 7-Eleven, train stations, or online. Register it for student concession (if eligible) and top it up for public transport in Victoria.

🛒 Groceries & Essentials

Find local supermarkets like Coles, Woolworths, and Aldi. Explore Indian/Asian grocery stores nearby for home-country items.

📍 Google Maps & Navigation

Download Google Maps and Public Transport Victoria (PTV) app for easy travel. Explore your suburb and learn where essential services are located.
