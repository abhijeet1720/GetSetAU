Jobs & Resume Tips

Where to Find Student Jobs

  • Online Job Portals: Seek, Indeed, Jora, Gumtree, Airtasker
  • University Career Centers: Regularly check your uni’s job boards and attend career fairs.
  • Local Businesses: Retail shops, cafes, restaurants often hire students for part-time roles.
  • Delivery & Ride Sharing: UberEats, DoorDash, Menulog offer flexible shifts.

Resume Writing Tips

  • Keep your resume concise (1 page).
  • Highlight your skills, education, and any work or volunteer experience.
  • Use action words like “Organized,” “Managed,” “Assisted.”
  • Tailor your resume for each job application.
  • Proofread for grammar and spelling errors.

Common Part-Time Jobs for Students

  • Barista or Café Worker
  • Retail Sales Assistant
  • Warehouse Assistant
  • Customer Service Representative
  • Delivery Driver or Courier
