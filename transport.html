Transport & Myki Card

What is Myki?

Myki is the reusable smart card used for public transport fares in Melbourne and some parts of Victoria.

It works on trains, trams, and buses.

How to Get a Myki Card

  • Buy one at train stations, 7-Eleven stores, or online at ptv.vic.gov.au
  • Register your card online to protect your balance
  • Top-up online or at retail outlets

Student Concession

If you’re an eligible student, you can apply for a concession card which allows discounted fares on Myki.

Check with your university for a valid student ID and concession application process.

Public Transport Tips

  • Use the PTV app to plan your routes and get real-time updates.
  • Trams in the city center (Free Tram Zone) are free to ride!
  • Peak hour fares are higher; travel outside these times to save money.
  • Always touch on and touch off your Myki card to avoid fines.
